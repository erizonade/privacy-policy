Privacy Policy

Effective for the Trackly App
Last updated: December 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, store, and protect your information when you use the Trackly application (“App”, “we”, “our”).

By using Trackly, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue using the App.

1. Information We Collect

Trackly is designed as a personal finance and activity tracking application. The types of information processed may include:

1.1. Information You Enter Manually

  • Financial transaction records (income, expenses, categories, notes, etc.).
  • Other activity logs such as to-do lists, habits, daily journals, and important notes (if such features are enabled).
  • Account names, wallet names, and any preferences or settings you configure.

1.2. Technical (Non-Personal) Device Information

Automatically collected data may include:

  • Device type and operating system.
  • Trackly app version.
  • Error logs or crash reports (if enabled) to help improve app performance.

1.3. Sync Data (If Cloud Sync Is Enabled in the Future)

If Trackly offers cloud sync features in the future, some data may be uploaded to a server or third-party service:

  • Financial records and notes you choose to sync.
  • Account information required for login (email or phone number), if login becomes available.

Details about cloud sync will be provided when the feature launches.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for purposes such as:

  • Storing and displaying financial records and personal activity logs.
  • Calculating summaries such as total income, expenses, balances, and statistics.
  • Personalizing app experience (theme, language, layout preferences).
  • Improving app stability, performance, and features.
  • Developing new features based on user behavior and needs.

3. Data Storage and Location

  • Most of your data is stored locally on your device (e.g., in the app's internal database).
  • If cloud sync is enabled in the future, synced data may be stored on third-party servers.

We recommend regular backups if the backup/export feature is available to prevent data loss.

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

However, we may share or process data when necessary with:

  • Service providers assisting with hosting, analytics, error tracking, or infrastructure.
  • Legal authorities when required to comply with applicable laws or court orders.

We only share the minimum necessary data when needed.

5. Device Permissions

Depending on the features you use, Trackly may request permissions such as:

  • Storage – for exporting or importing backup files.
  • Notifications – for reminders about bills, goals, or activities.

You may disable permissions at any time, but doing so may limit certain features.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no digital system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against risks such as device loss, malware, or weak passwords.

You are encouraged to:

  • Use device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, face lock).
  • Protect backup files and avoid sharing sensitive information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored within the App.
  • Edit or delete your personal entries.
  • Uninstall the App at any time.

If available, you may also use export/backup tools to keep a copy of your data.

8. Children’s Privacy

Trackly is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us for assistance.
